Subic Ay! Festival


Date: April (This festival happens once every three years)


The festival was organized and spearheaded by the Municipal Government of Subic, Zambales in cooperation with the different non-government organizations of the town, and is being held in order to boost the tourism industry of the locality aside from instilling the spirit and pride of patriotism among the Subiquenos. The "word "AY! is the expression habitually and fondly used by the majority Tagalog speaking people of Subic, which makes the Subiqueno unique and distinct during conversations from other Tagalog speaking inhabitants of the region.


Events:
Street Dancing Contest
Drum and Lyre Competition
Subic Got Talent
Banca Race/Karakol Exhibit
Pasayaw ng Bayan
Binibining Subic
Subic Mardigras
Variety Show featuring local celebrities
