Measurement: 1100 MASL

Meaning "Mountatin of Thunder" in the native Aeta language Mt. Balingkilat, is one of the highest peaks in the Zambales Coastal Mountains and Coves area measured at 1100 MASL (Meters Above Sea Level), an emerging hiking hotspot within San Antonio and Subic towns that has scenic views and nice trails that interconnect high peaks and beautiful coves. If you want to visit/trek at Mt. Balingkilat, Please contact the Subic Police Department: (047) 232-2600 and be sure to present a letter of request.

Jump Off: Sito Cawag, Subic, Zambales 2209
