Mamalakaya Festival


Date: July 25 every year


Mamalakaya festival is an annual celebration of the feast of the town's patron saint, St. James the Apostle. Celebrated July 25 every year, the festival includes a drum and lyre competition, mass fluvial parade during the day and a procession at night.


Events:
Drum and Lyre Competition
Mass
Fluvial Parade
Procession
